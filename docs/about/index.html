About TradieTools NZ

Free calculators, guides, and tools built by NZ tradespeople — for NZ tradespeople.

Who built this?

TradieTools NZ was founded by Benjamen, an Auckland-based trade business owner who got frustrated with the lack of practical, NZ-specific tools for running a trade business. Every calculator, rate table, and tax guide on this site solves a problem we actually faced.

We are not accountants, lawyers, or tax advisors — but we have spent years navigating NZ's GST rules, provisional tax cycles, ACC levies, and quoting practices. Our guides are written to explain things in plain English, not to replace professional advice for complex situations.

How we research

  • Rate data — tradie hourly rates are compiled from job listings, trade forums, subcontractor agreements, and direct input from NZ tradespeople across 20 cities
  • Tax information — verified against IRD's official guidance. We flag when rules changed and update pages when IRD releases new rates or thresholds
  • Calculators — formulas are based on NZ legislation (GST Act 1985, Income Tax Act 2007) and cross-checked against IRD calculators where they exist
  • H&S content — referenced against WorkSafe NZ guidelines and the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015
  • Community review — active in NZ trade Facebook groups; real-world feedback from tradies is incorporated into guides regularly
Disclaimer: TradieTools NZ provides general information only. For tax advice specific to your situation, consult a registered NZ tax agent or accountant. For legal advice, contact a solicitor. IRD tax rates, thresholds, and deadlines change — always confirm current figures at ird.govt.nz.
