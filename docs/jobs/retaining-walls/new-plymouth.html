Typical NZ prices for retaining walls in New Plymouth — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

Retaining Walls Price Guide — New Plymouth 2026

ScopeEstimated Cost (New Plymouth)What's Typical

🧮 Is Your Quote in the Right Range?

✅ Typically Included

Labour, materials (timber/block/stone), drainage behind wall, and concrete footings.

❌ Usually Extra

Building consent for walls over 1.5m high, earthworks and soil disposal.

Tips for Hiring in New Plymouth

  • Walls over 1.5m require building consent in most NZ councils
  • Always include drainage behind the wall to prevent failure
  • Concrete block outlasts timber but costs more upfront
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in New Plymouth can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
