What's Included in a Typical Retaining Walls Quote?

Usually included: Labour, materials (timber/block/stone), drainage behind wall, and concrete footings.

Usually extra: Building consent for walls over 1.5m high, earthworks and soil disposal.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Short timber wall (≤6m, up to 1m high) $3,000 – $7,500
Medium block wall (6–20m, 1–1.5m high) $10,000 – $25,000
Large engineered wall (20m+, 1.5m+ high) $28,000 – $70,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Walls over 1.5m require building consent in most NZ councils
  • Always include drainage behind the wall to prevent failure
  • Concrete block outlasts timber but costs more upfront
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
