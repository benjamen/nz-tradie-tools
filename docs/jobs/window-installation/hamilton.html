🧮 Is Your Quote in the Right Range?

✅ Typically Included

Supply of uPVC or aluminium double-glazed windows, removal of old windows, installation, interior and exterior trims.

❌ Usually Extra

Structural alterations, consent fees (if opening sizes change), internal painting, curtain tracks.

Tips for Hiring in Hamilton

  • Double-glazing is mandatory for new builds under NZ Building Code — worth upgrading if replacing single glazing
  • Aluminium joinery is most common in NZ; uPVC offers better thermal performance
  • Get written confirmation of the glazing unit's energy rating
  • Low-e glass adds cost but significantly reduces heat loss in cold climates
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Hamilton can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
