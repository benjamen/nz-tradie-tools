🎨 Interior & Exterior Painting Cost in New Plymouth (2026)

Typical NZ prices for interior & exterior painting in New Plymouth — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

Taranaki's main city benefits from oil and gas industry money, supporting a well-paid workforce and strong demand for quality residential work. A good supply of experienced tradies. Professional interior or exterior painting including preparation and two coats.

Interior & Exterior Painting Price Guide — New Plymouth 2026

ScopeEstimated Cost (New Plymouth)What's Typical

🧮 Is Your Quote in the Right Range?

✅ Typically Included

Surface preparation, filling, sanding, primer where needed, two coats of finish paint.

❌ Usually Extra

Wallpaper removal (extra charge), plaster repair beyond minor filling.

Tips for Hiring in New Plymouth

  • Good preparation is 80% of a quality paint job
  • Ask for a specification listing paint brand and sheen
  • Exterior repaints every 8–10 years extend cladding life significantly
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in New Plymouth can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
