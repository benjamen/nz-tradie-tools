🚧 Fencing & Gates Cost in Auckland (2026)

Typical NZ prices for fencing & gates in Auckland — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

New Zealand's largest city and economic hub, Auckland has the most tradies and the most competitive market. Expect higher hourly rates reflecting the city's higher cost of living. New fence installation including posts, rails, and palings or panels.

Tips for Hiring in Auckland

  • Check boundary lines with your council before starting
  • Timber palings are cheapest but need treatment or painting
  • Get neighbour agreement in writing for boundary fences
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Auckland can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
