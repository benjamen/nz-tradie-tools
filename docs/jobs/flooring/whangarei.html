Tips for Hiring in Whangārei

  • Vinyl plank is the best value for wet areas like bathrooms and laundries
  • Engineered timber is more stable than solid timber in NZ's climate
  • Get subfloor checked for moisture before installing timber
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Whangārei can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
