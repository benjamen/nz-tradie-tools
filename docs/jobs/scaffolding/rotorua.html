Tips for Hiring in Rotorua

  • Scaffolding is required under NZ H&S rules for any work above 3m
  • Get quotes with clear weekly hire rates — projects often run over time
  • Scaffold must be erected and dismantled by a competent person
  • For painters: include scaffold cost in your quote to avoid disputes with clients
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Rotorua can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
