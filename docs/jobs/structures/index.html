What's Included in a Typical Garages & Sleepouts Quote?

Usually included: Foundation/slab, framing, roofing, cladding, windows, and door to lockup stage.

Usually extra: Building consent, internal lining/fitout, electrical, plumbing.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Single garage or carport (≤25m²) $15,000 – $30,000
Double garage or sleepout (25–50m²) $30,000 – $60,000
Large garage-workshop or studio (50m²+) $60,000 – $110,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Most garages and sleepouts require building consent — allow 4–8 weeks
  • Prefab kitset garages are cheaper but require consent if attached to house
  • Insulate if used as a sleepout — NZ building code requires it
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
