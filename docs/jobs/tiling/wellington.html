Tips for Hiring in Wellington

  • Always use a licensed waterproofer in wet areas before tiling — required under NZ Building Code
  • Large format tiles (600×600+) need a very flat substrate — check and level before tiling
  • Porcelain tiles are durable and low-maintenance but harder to cut — factor in extra time
  • Budget 10–20% extra tiles for wastage, especially with patterns or diagonals
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Wellington can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
