Typical NZ prices for tiling (floor & wall) in Dunedin — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

Otago's main city has a mix of older character homes and new developments. The student population creates year-round demand for maintenance work. Rates are among the most affordable in NZ. Professional floor and wall tiling for bathrooms, kitchens, laundries, and living areas.

Tips for Hiring in Dunedin

  • Always use a licensed waterproofer in wet areas before tiling — required under NZ Building Code
  • Large format tiles (600×600+) need a very flat substrate — check and level before tiling
  • Porcelain tiles are durable and low-maintenance but harder to cut — factor in extra time
  • Budget 10–20% extra tiles for wastage, especially with patterns or diagonals
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Dunedin can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
