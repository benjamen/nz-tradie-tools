What's Included in a Typical Landscaping Quote?

Usually included: Labour, topsoil, plants, lawn, garden paths, and irrigation if required.

Usually extra: Tree removal, major earthworks and consent for large structures.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Small garden makeover (≤60m²) $3,000 – $9,000
Medium landscaping project (60–200m²) $10,000 – $28,000
Full property landscaping (200m²+) $30,000 – $80,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Hire a landscape designer for complex projects — saves cost in the long run
  • Native plants require less maintenance and suit NZ conditions
  • Ask if topsoil and plants are included in the quote
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
