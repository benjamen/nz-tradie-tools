🏗️ External Cladding Cost in Hastings (2026)

Typical NZ prices for external cladding in Hastings — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

Hastings serves the Hawke's Bay horticultural region and shares many tradies with nearby Napier. Strong demand for new residential builds and commercial work. External wall cladding replacement or installation including weatherboards, Linea, or Hardie.

External Cladding Price Guide — Hastings 2026

ScopeEstimated Cost (Hastings)What's Typical
One or two elevations (≤50m²) $8,550 – $17,100 inc GST
Partial re-clad (50–130m², Linea/Hardie) $20,900 – $42,750 inc GST
Full home re-clad (130m²+) $47,500 – $95,000 inc GST

✅ Typically Included

Tips for Hiring in Hastings

  • Leaky home era (1994–2004) homes must be fully recladded if using direct-fix
  • Cavity-drain cladding systems are now standard — avoid direct-fix
  • James Hardie and weathertex are popular mid-range cladding options in NZ
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Hastings can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
