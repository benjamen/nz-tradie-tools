What's Included in a Typical Pest Control Quote?

Usually included: Treatment, baiting/trapping (where applicable), report, follow-up visit (usually included).

Usually extra: Structural repairs caused by pests, large-scale rodent proofing, ongoing annual programme.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Single treatment (ants, cockroaches, fleas) $150 – $400
Full property treatment + report $380 – $750
Annual programme or commercial property $800 – $3,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Use a pest control operator with a current licence from the NZPMA
  • Pre-purchase building inspections should always include a pest inspection
  • Ongoing programmes are more cost-effective for rural properties or those near bush
  • Borer treatment for timber: treat in summer when larvae are active near the surface
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
