Tips for Hiring in Gisborne

  • Decks over 1.5m above ground require building consent
  • Hardwood and composite last longer but cost more upfront
  • Get a fixed-price quote to avoid cost blowout
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Gisborne can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
