What's Included in a Typical Deck Building Quote?

Usually included: Consent (if needed), framing, posts, decking boards, fixings, and basic balustrades.

Usually extra: Painting or staining (optional extra), pergola or shade structure.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Small deck (≤20m², treated pine) $6,000 – $13,000
Medium deck (20–40m², hardwood) $14,000 – $30,000
Large deck (40m²+, composite/hardwood) $32,000 – $65,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Decks over 1.5m above ground require building consent
  • Hardwood and composite last longer but cost more upfront
  • Get a fixed-price quote to avoid cost blowout
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
