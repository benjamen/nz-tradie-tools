Tips for Hiring in Tauranga

  • Replace gutters every 20–30 years or when they start sagging or leaking
  • Aluminium gutters are the standard in NZ — avoid galvanised in coastal areas
  • Check downpipes run to proper stormwater drain, not just the garden
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Tauranga can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
