Tips for Hiring in Porirua

  • LED downlights save significant power over halogen
  • Ensure your electrician is registered with the Electrical Workers Registration Board
  • Dimmer switches add cost but improve comfort and save power
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Porirua can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
