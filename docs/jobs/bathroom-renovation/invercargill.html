Tips for Hiring in Invercargill

  • Get a licensed plumber for all drainage and water supply work
  • Budget 10–15% extra for unexpected issues behind walls
  • Choose fittings before quoting so labour scope is clear
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Invercargill can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
