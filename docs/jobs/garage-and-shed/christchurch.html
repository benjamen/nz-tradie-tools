Tips for Hiring in Christchurch

  • Building consent is required for any garage over 10m² in NZ — factor in 6–12 weeks for consent
  • Concrete floor thickness should be 100mm minimum for a residential garage
  • Kit-set garages can be cost-effective but still require a licensed builder for foundations and consent
  • Consider insulation from the start — much cheaper than retrofitting
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Christchurch can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
