Tips for Hiring in Wellington

  • Heat pumps are the most cost-effective heating in NZ — 3:1 efficiency ratio
  • Check your council rules on wood burner installation (many urban areas restrict)
  • HRV or DVS systems are worth it in damp NZ homes
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Wellington can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
