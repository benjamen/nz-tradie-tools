Tips for Hiring in Nelson

  • Concrete outlasts asphalt by 15–20 years in NZ conditions
  • Exposed aggregate or brushed concrete adds grip and looks better
  • Get at least 2 quotes — driveway pricing varies significantly
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Nelson can vary 30–40%.
