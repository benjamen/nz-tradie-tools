Typical NZ prices for driveway construction in Whangārei — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

Tips for Hiring in Whangārei

  • Concrete outlasts asphalt by 15–20 years in NZ conditions
  • Exposed aggregate or brushed concrete adds grip and looks better
  • Get at least 2 quotes — driveway pricing varies significantly
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Whangārei can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
