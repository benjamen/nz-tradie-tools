🧮 Is Your Quote in the Right Range?

✅ Typically Included

Pool shell, excavation, filtration system, pool fencing (mandatory NZ), basic surrounds.

❌ Usually Extra

Building consent, decking/paving surrounds, heating, pool cover, ongoing chemicals.

Tips for Hiring in New Plymouth

  • Pool fencing is mandatory in NZ — must comply with Building Code clause F9, self-closing gate required
  • Building consent is required for all residential pools
  • Fibreglass pools are fastest to install; concrete offers most customisation
  • Consider an energy-efficient heat pump for heating — gas is expensive to run long-term
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in New Plymouth can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
