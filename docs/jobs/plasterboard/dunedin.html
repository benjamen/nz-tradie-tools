Tips for Hiring in Dunedin

  • Level 4 stopping is standard for painted surfaces in NZ
  • Ensure stopping is fully dry before painting — rush jobs cause cracking
  • GIB Aqualine or equivalent is required in wet areas
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in Dunedin can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
