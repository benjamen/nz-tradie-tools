What's Included in a Typical Roof Replacement Quote?

Usually included: Removal of old roof, underlay, all flashings, new roofing material and fixings.

Usually extra: Guttering (quoted separately), fascia/soffit repairs, roof painting.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Small home roof (≤100m², iron/Colorsteel) $12,000 – $22,000
Average home roof (100–180m², Colorsteel) $22,000 – $40,000
Large home roof (180m²+, Colorsteel/tile) $40,000 – $75,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Get quotes from licensed roofing contractors only
  • Colorsteel is the most cost-effective option in NZ
  • Consider insulation upgrade while the roof is off
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
