What's Included in a Typical Kitchen Renovation Quote?

Usually included: Labour, cabinetry, benchtop, splashback, plumbing connections, basic electrical.

Usually extra: Appliances (unless specified), structural changes, consent fees.

NZ Price Ranges (Baseline — Christchurch)

ScopeEstimated Cost
Minor refresh (new benchtop/splashback only) $5,000 – $12,000
Full renovation (new cabinets, bench, appliances) $18,000 – $40,000
Premium fit-out (stone bench, custom cabinetry) $45,000 – $100,000

Christchurch baseline — apply city multiplier for your region. Auckland is ~15% higher; Queenstown ~20% higher; Invercargill/Whanganui ~10% lower.

Tips for Getting the Best Quote

  • Get separate quotes for cabinetry, plumbing, and electrical
  • Allow 6–12 weeks lead time for custom cabinetry
  • Use a project manager for full kitchen gut-and-redo
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes before committing.
  • Check licence on the relevant NZ register.
