🍳 Kitchen Renovation Cost in New Plymouth (2026)

Typical NZ prices for kitchen renovation in New Plymouth — updated 2026. Based on real quotes from NZ tradies.

Taranaki's main city benefits from oil and gas industry money, supporting a well-paid workforce and strong demand for quality residential work. A good supply of experienced tradies. Kitchen renovation including cabinetry, benchtop, splashback, and connections.

Kitchen Renovation Price Guide — New Plymouth 2026

ScopeEstimated Cost (New Plymouth)What's Typical
Minor refresh (new benchtop/splashback only) $4,700 – $11,280 inc GST
Full renovation (new cabinets, bench, appliances) $16,920 – $37,600 inc GST
Premium fit-out (stone bench, custom cabinetry) $42,300 – $94,000 inc GST

Tips for Hiring in New Plymouth

  • Get separate quotes for cabinetry, plumbing, and electrical
  • Allow 6–12 weeks lead time for custom cabinetry
  • Use a project manager for full kitchen gut-and-redo
  • Always get at least 3 written quotes — prices in New Plymouth can vary 30–40%.
  • Check the tradie's licence on the relevant NZ register before signing anything.
