Hiring a Tradie in Christchurch: What to Know

Canterbury's largest city has seen significant rebuild activity since the 2011 earthquake, creating a large and experienced tradie workforce. Rates are generally lower than Auckland and Wellington.

When hiring any tradie in Christchurch, always:

  • Get at least 3 written quotes
  • Check their licence or registration on the relevant register
  • Ask for references from recent similar jobs in Christchurch
  • Never pay the full amount upfront — a deposit of 10–20% is normal
  • Make sure they have public liability insurance
