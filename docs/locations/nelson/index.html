Hiring a Tradie in Nelson: What to Know

Nelson's sunny climate and lifestyle appeal attract retirees and lifestyle block buyers, creating steady renovation and new build demand. A strong arts and creative community also drives unique fitout work.

When hiring any tradie in Nelson, always:

  • Get at least 3 written quotes
  • Check their licence or registration on the relevant register
  • Ask for references from recent similar jobs in Nelson
  • Never pay the full amount upfront — a deposit of 10–20% is normal
  • Make sure they have public liability insurance
