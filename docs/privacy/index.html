Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

Who We Are

NZ Tradie Tools (tradietools.nz) provides free calculators, guides, and tradie directories for the New Zealand trades industry.

What Data We Collect

Forms (Formspree)

When you submit a form on this site (e.g. to request free templates), your name and email address are collected via Formspree. We use this only to send you the requested resource and occasional relevant updates. We do not sell your data.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site (pages visited, time on site, general location). This data is anonymised and aggregated. You can opt out via your browser's privacy settings or a browser extension.

Advertising

This site may display Google AdSense advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ad Settings.

Affiliate Links

Some links on this site are affiliate links — if you click through and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend tools and services we believe are genuinely useful to NZ tradies.

Cookies

This site uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. By continuing to use the site you consent to this. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

Third-Party Sites

This site links to external websites (Google Maps, No Cowboys, trade directories, software providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under the NZ Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access and correct personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at instituteofbba@gmail.com.

Questions about this policy? Email us at instituteofbba@gmail.com.
