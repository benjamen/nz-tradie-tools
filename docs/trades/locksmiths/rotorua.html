How to Choose a Locksmith in Rotorua

  • MLNZ (Master Locksmiths NZ) membership
  • Lock grade specification (Grade 1/2/3)
  • Key control documentation for master systems
  • Transparent call-out and labour rates
  • After-hours rate clearly stated

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't show ID before entering
  • No pricing transparency
  • Can't provide lock grade information
  • Extremely high after-hours charges without disclosure
