Napier Bathroom Renovators Pricing Guide

Bathroom renovations in Napier typically cost $9,000–$17,000 for a basic refresh, $20,000–$38,000 for a mid-range full reno, and $38,000–$85,000+ for premium work. Hawke's Bay rates typically run 5–10% below the national average. Waterproofing, tiling, and plumbing are the largest cost components — confirm all are included in any fixed-price quote.

How to Choose a Bathroom Renovator in Napier

  • Full scope including waterproofing specification
  • Coordinates all sub-trades (plumber, tiler, electrician)
  • Building Code E3 waterproofing compliance
  • Fixture allowance clearly documented
  • Timeline and progress payment schedule

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Waterproofing not specifically addressed
  • Won't coordinate sub-trades
  • No fixture allowance detail
  • Very large upfront deposit
