Whangārei Roofers & Roof Tilers Pricing Guide

Whangārei roofers charge $80–$110/hr. Coastal and Northland humidity means anti-corrosion coatings are especially important.

Use our free Job Cost Calculator to check if a quote is reasonable for your job.

How to Choose a Roofer in Whangārei

  • RANZ membership for quality assurance
  • Experience with your specific roof type (Colorsteel, concrete tile, membrane)
  • Public liability insurance
  • 10-year workmanship guarantee
  • Product and manufacturer warranties supplied in writing

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Asks for full payment before work begins
  • Can't show photos of similar past jobs
  • No written quote with specifications
  • Won't provide a workmanship guarantee

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing, but look for members of the Roofing Association of NZ (RANZ). LBP required for structural roof changes. Verify a licence →

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do roofers cost in Whangārei?

Whangārei roofers charge $80–$110/hr. Coastal and Northland humidity means anti-corrosion coatings are especially important. Always get at least 3 quotes before committing.

Do roofers need a licence in NZ?

No mandatory licensing, but look for members of the Roofing Association of NZ (RANZ). LBP required for structural roof changes. Check the register →

How many quotes should I get for a roofer job in Whangārei?

Always get at least 3 written quotes from licensed tradespeople. Compare the scope of work, not just the price — the cheapest quote often excludes items the others include. Use our Job Cost Calculator to sense-check any quote.

What's the best way to find a reliable roofer in Whangārei?

Start with Google Maps reviews (filter by highest rated, minimum 20 reviews), then check No Cowboys for verified NZ reviews. Always confirm their licence before signing anything.
