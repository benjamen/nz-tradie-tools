Whangārei Painters & Decorators Pricing Guide

Whangārei painters charge $55–$80/hr. Coastal proximity means exterior paint systems need to handle salt air — spec accordingly.

Use our free Job Cost Calculator to check if a quote is reasonable for your job.

How to Choose a Painter in Whangārei

  • PDANZ membership (voluntary but signals quality)
  • Public liability insurance
  • References and photos of recent work
  • Detailed quote specifying paint brand, number of coats, preparation work
  • Experience with your surface type (weatherboard, plaster, etc.)

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Quote doesn't specify prep work
  • Won't specify paint brand or quality
  • No references
  • Asks for most payment upfront

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing in NZ, but look for members of the Painting and Decorating Association NZ (PDANZ). Verify a licence →

Frequently Asked Questions

Do painters need a licence in NZ?

How many quotes should I get for a painter job in Whangārei?
