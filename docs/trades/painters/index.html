What to Look for When Hiring a Painter in NZ

  • PDANZ membership (voluntary but signals quality)
  • Public liability insurance
  • References and photos of recent work
  • Detailed quote specifying paint brand, number of coats, preparation work
  • Experience with your surface type (weatherboard, plaster, etc.)

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Quote doesn't specify prep work
  • Won't specify paint brand or quality
  • No references
  • Asks for most payment upfront

Typical Costs Across NZ

$55–$80/hr, or $400–$700/day. Full interior house repaint: $3,000–$8,000+

Costs vary significantly by city — Auckland and Queenstown are typically the most expensive, while Invercargill and Dunedin are among the most affordable.

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing in NZ, but look for members of the Painting and Decorating Association NZ (PDANZ). Check the register →
