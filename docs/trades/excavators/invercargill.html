Invercargill Excavation & Earthworks Contractors Pricing Guide

Excavation in Invercargill typically costs $185–$230/hr with operator, $38–$70/m³ disposed for a site cut, and $900–$3,500 for foundation excavation. Southland rates are typically 10–15% below the national average, though remote work may attract travel charges. Confirm spoil disposal is included — tipping fees and cartage can add significantly to the cost.
