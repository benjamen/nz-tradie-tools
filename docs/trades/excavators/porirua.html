Porirua Excavation & Earthworks Contractors Pricing Guide

Excavation in Porirua typically costs $185–$230/hr with operator, $38–$70/m³ disposed for a site cut, and $900–$3,500 for foundation excavation. Porirua trades often price at Wellington rates given the shared labour pool. Confirm spoil disposal is included — tipping fees and cartage can add significantly to the cost.

How to Choose a Excavation Contractor in Porirua

  • Resource consent compliance if required
  • Spoil disposal plan included
  • Utility location (Call Before You Dig) process

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No spoil disposal plan
