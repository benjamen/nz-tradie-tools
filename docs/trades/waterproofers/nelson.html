Nelson Waterproofers Pricing Guide

Nelson waterproofing contractors typically charge $900–$2,800 for bathroom waterproofing, $85–$190/m² for deck membranes, and $65–$140/m² for roof membranes. Nelson/Tasman rates are broadly in line with the national average. Always use a licensed waterproofer for wet areas — poor waterproofing is one of the most common and costly defects in NZ homes.

How to Choose a Waterproofer in Nelson

  • Specific membrane system named (Sika, Ardex, Mapei etc)
  • Manufacturer's approved applicator status
  • Primer and detailing spec in quote
  • Flood test before tiling
  • Building Code E2/E3 compliance certification

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't name the membrane product
  • No flood test offered
  • No manufacturer warranty
  • Experience only with residential (not your commercial job type)
