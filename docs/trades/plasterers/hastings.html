How to Choose a Plasterer in Hastings

  • Experience with your specific system (GIB, plaster, texture coat)
  • Knowledge of E2 (External Moisture) requirements for exterior work
  • References from similar projects
  • Specification of products (primer, texture, topcoat)
  • Clean and tidy work practices

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Can't specify the product system being used
  • No experience with exterior cladding waterproofing
  • Won't provide a written quote
  • No references
