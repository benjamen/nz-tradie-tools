Dunedin Plumbers & Gasfitters Pricing Guide

Plumbers in Dunedin typically charge $95–$120/hr for standard residential work. Emergency and after-hours callouts add $150–$250 to the bill. Dunedin’s cold winters and ageing housing stock mean burst pipes and heating system failures are common — budgeting for a regular service check is worthwhile.

How to Choose a Plumber in Dunedin

  • Current registration with the PGD Board
  • Licensed for the specific work type (plumbing, gasfitting, or drainlaying)
  • Public liability insurance
  • Responds promptly to urgent callouts
  • Upfront pricing or clear hourly rate

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Can't provide registration number
  • Quotes suspiciously low for gas work
  • No insurance
  • Doesn't provide a written invoice

Licensing Requirements

Must be registered and licensed under the Plumbers, Gasfitters and Drainlayers Act 2006. Check the PGD Board register. Verify a licence →
