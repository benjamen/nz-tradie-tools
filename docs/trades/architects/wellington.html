How to Choose a Architect in Wellington

  • NZRAB registration
  • Portfolio of similar project types
  • Clear scope of services (concept, consents, contract administration)
  • Fee structure (percentage, fixed, or time-based)
  • Insurance (professional indemnity)

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Not NZRAB registered
  • Vague fee structure
  • No professional indemnity insurance
  • No portfolio of similar work
