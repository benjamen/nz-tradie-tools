How to Choose a Gasfitter in Nelson

  • Specific gasfitting licence (separate from plumbing)
  • Experience with your gas type (LPG or natural gas)
  • Issues a Gas Certificate of Compliance
  • Familiarity with AS/NZS 5601 gas installation code
  • Public liability insurance

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Can't show gasfitting licence (not just plumbing)
  • Won't provide a Certificate of Compliance
  • Quotes very low for gas line work
  • No insurance

Licensing Requirements
