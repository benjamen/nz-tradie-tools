How to Choose a Glazier in Whanganui

  • Glass specification (toughened, laminated, double-glazed) in writing
  • Building Code F4 compliance for safety glass locations
  • NZGMA membership
  • Experience with your glass type
  • Correct weather seals and flashing included

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't specify glass type
  • Standard float glass in safety locations
  • No mention of building code compliance
  • Won't warranty their work
