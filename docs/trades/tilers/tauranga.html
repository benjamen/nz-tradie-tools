Tauranga Tilers Pricing Guide

Tauranga tiling $70–$100/m²; coastal properties favour non-slip outdoor tiles.

Use our free Job Cost Calculator to check if a quote is reasonable for your job.

How to Choose a Tiler in Tauranga

  • Experience with your tile type (large format, mosaic, outdoor)
  • Waterproofing membrane specification included
  • Adhesive and grout product details in quote
  • Layout plan before starting
  • References from bathroom/wet area projects

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No waterproofing mentioned for wet areas
  • Can't specify adhesive or grout products
  • Quote per m² only — no detail on prep
  • Won't do a layout plan

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing. Waterproofing must comply with NZ Building Code E3. Verify a licence →
