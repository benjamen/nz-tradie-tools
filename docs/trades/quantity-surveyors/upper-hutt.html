Upper Hutt Quantity Surveyors Pricing Guide

Quantity surveyors in Upper Hutt typically charge $2,200–$9,000 for a construction cost estimate and 1–3% of construction cost for contract administration. Upper Hutt trades often price at Wellington rates given the shared labour pool. A QS is most valuable on projects over $200,000 — they typically save more than their fee through better cost control and contract management.

How to Choose a Quantity Surveyor in Upper Hutt

  • NZIQS membership (PQS or AIQS equivalent)
  • Experience with your project type (residential, commercial, civil)
  • Elemental cost plan format
  • Tender evaluation included
  • Professional indemnity insurance

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Not NZIQS-affiliated
  • Estimate without site visit
  • No professional indemnity insurance
  • Vague scope of services
