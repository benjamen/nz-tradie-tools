New Plymouth Electricians Pricing Guide

Electricians in New Plymouth typically charge $75–$105/hr + GST for a registered/qualified electrician. Emergency callouts and after-hours work attract surcharges. Taranaki regional rates sit below Auckland/Wellington ($95–$130/hr + GST).

Use our free Job Cost Calculator to check if a quote is reasonable for your job.

How to Choose a Electrician in New Plymouth

  • Registered Electrical Worker (REW) licence
  • Issues an Electrical Certificate of Compliance (CoC) after work
  • Public liability insurance
  • Familiar with NZ electrical standards (AS/NZS 3000)
  • Experience with your specific job type (domestic, commercial, heat pumps)

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Can't provide REW registration number
  • Doesn't mention a Certificate of Compliance
  • Quotes for work without seeing the site
  • No insurance certificate

Licensing Requirements

Must be a Registered Electrical Worker (REW) under the Electricity Act 1992. All electrical work must be certified. Verify a licence →
