What to Look for When Hiring a Pool Builder in NZ

  • Building consent included in quote
  • Pool fence compliance certification
  • Manufacturer warranty (fibreglass shell, filtration)
  • Site visit and soil assessment
  • Reference from similar installations

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Building consent not included
  • No fence compliance mentioned
  • No soil assessment for in-ground pool
  • Very low quote without specification

Typical Costs Across NZ

Fibreglass pool: $35,000–$70,000 installed. Concrete/gunite: $50,000–$120,000+. Above-ground: $3,000–$12,000

Costs vary significantly by city — Auckland and Queenstown are typically the most expensive, while Invercargill and Dunedin are among the most affordable.

Licensing Requirements

Building consent required for all pools. Pool fence must comply with the Fencing of Swimming Pools Act 1987. Check the register →
