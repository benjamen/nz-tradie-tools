How to Choose a Landscaper in Lower Hutt

  • Portfolio of similar completed projects
  • Knowledge of NZ native plants and local soils
  • Public liability insurance
  • Clear scope with plant species and quantities specified
  • Aftercare advice and plant warranties

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Can't show portfolio
  • No soil preparation included in quote
