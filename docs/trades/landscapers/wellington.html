Wellington Landscapers & Garden Designers Pricing Guide

Wellington landscaping $80–$115/hr; exposed sites add 15–20% for wind-rated plantings.

How to Choose a Landscaper in Wellington

  • Portfolio of similar completed projects
  • Knowledge of NZ native plants and local soils
  • Public liability insurance
  • Clear scope with plant species and quantities specified
  • Aftercare advice and plant warranties

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No plant species specified in quote
  • Can't show portfolio
  • No soil preparation included in quote
  • No aftercare advice

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing. Look for members of Landscape NZ (formerly NZILA/LAND8). Verify a licence →

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do landscapers cost in Wellington?

Wellington landscaping $80–$115/hr; exposed sites add 15–20% for wind-rated plantings. Always get at least 3 quotes before committing.

Do landscapers need a licence in NZ?

No mandatory licensing. Look for members of Landscape NZ (formerly NZILA/LAND8). Check the register →

How many quotes should I get for a landscaper job in Wellington?

Always get at least 3 written quotes from licensed tradespeople. Compare the scope of work, not just the price — the cheapest quote often excludes items the others include. Use our Job Cost Calculator to sense-check any quote.

What's the best way to find a reliable landscaper in Wellington?

Start with Google Maps reviews (filter by highest rated, minimum 20 reviews), then check No Cowboys for verified NZ reviews. Always confirm their licence before signing anything.
