Upper Hutt Bricklayers & Blocklayers Pricing Guide

Bricklayers in Upper Hutt typically charge $125–$190/m² for single-skin brickwork, $115–$170/m² for blockwork, and $210–$420/m² for heritage restoration. Upper Hutt trades often price at Wellington rates given the shared labour pool. Brick is less common in NZ new builds but remains popular for retaining walls, paving, and heritage restoration.

How to Choose a Bricklayer in Upper Hutt

  • LBP credentials for structural work
  • Mortar mix specification
  • DPC (damp proof course) detail
  • Movement joint design
  • Experience with your brick or block type

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No mortar mix specified
  • No DPC mentioned for ground-level walls
  • No movement joints in long walls
  • Can't provide LBP for structural work
