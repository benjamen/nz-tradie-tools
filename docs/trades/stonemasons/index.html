What to Look for When Hiring a Stonemason in NZ

  • Portfolio of stone work matching your project
  • Mortar specification compatible with the stone type
  • LBP for structural work
  • Experience with NZ stone (schist, bluestone, granite)
  • Drainage design for dry-stack walls

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Incompatible mortar mix for stone type
  • No experience with your stone material
  • No LBP for structural walls
  • No drainage detail for retaining walls

Typical Costs Across NZ

Natural stone wall: $350–$700/m² installed. Stone paving: $200–$450/m². Heritage restoration: $400–$900/m²

Costs vary significantly by city — Auckland and Queenstown are typically the most expensive, while Invercargill and Dunedin are among the most affordable.

Licensing Requirements

LBP required for structural stone work. Heritage work may require special consent. Check the register →
