Whanganui Asphalt & Driveway Contractors Pricing Guide

Asphalt contractors in Whanganui typically charge $85–$140/m² for an asphalt driveway and $28–$60/m² for chip seal. Whanganui rates are typically 10–15% below the national average. Get a written quote that specifies base preparation — a well-prepared sub-base is the key to a long-lasting driveway.

How to Choose a Asphalt Contractor in Whanganui

  • Base course specification and depth
  • Asphalt depth and grade specified
  • Drainage design (crossfall)
  • Edge restraints or kerbing
  • Warranty on workmanship

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No base course preparation
  • Won't specify asphalt depth
  • No drainage design
  • Very cheap quote (thin asphalt, no base)
