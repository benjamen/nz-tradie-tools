Napier Timber Floor Specialists Pricing Guide

Timber floor specialists in Napier typically charge $155–$295/m² for solid hardwood supply and install, and $28–$60/m² for sanding and polishing existing floors. Hawke's Bay rates typically run 5–10% below the national average. Engineered timber floors are often more cost-effective in areas prone to humidity changes.

How to Choose a Timber Floor Specialist in Napier

  • Timber species and grade specification
  • Moisture testing of subfloor before installation
  • Acclimatisation period for solid timber
  • Coating system (oil, polyurethane, wax) specified
  • NZFA membership

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No moisture check before installation
  • Won't specify coating system
  • No acclimatisation period for solid timber
  • Unknown timber source or species
