How to Choose a Lighting Specialist in Hastings

  • REW registration for all installation work
  • Lux level design for commercial spaces
  • LED brand specification (Philips, LIFX, Lutron)
  • Smart lighting and DALI control experience
  • Energy savings calculations provided

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No REW registration
  • Generic unbranded LED fittings
  • No lux design for commercial
  • Smart system incompatible with your existing platform
