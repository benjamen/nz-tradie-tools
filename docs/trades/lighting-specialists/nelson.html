Nelson Lighting Designers & Installers Pricing Guide

Lighting specialists in Nelson typically charge $2,600–$6,500 for a full-house LED upgrade (supply and install), $420–$1,900/circuit for outdoor lighting, and $155–$265/hr for commercial lighting design. Nelson/Tasman rates are broadly in line with the national average. All electrical work must be done by a registered electrician — lighting specialists who aren't electricians can only advise, not install.

How to Choose a Lighting Specialist in Nelson

  • REW registration for all installation work
  • Lux level design for commercial spaces
  • LED brand specification (Philips, LIFX, Lutron)
  • Smart lighting and DALI control experience
  • Energy savings calculations provided

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No REW registration
  • Generic unbranded LED fittings
  • No lux design for commercial
  • Smart system incompatible with your existing platform
