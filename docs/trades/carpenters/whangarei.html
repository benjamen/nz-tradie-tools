How to Choose a Carpenter in Whangārei

  • LBP licence for any structural work
  • Portfolio of custom joinery or decking projects
  • Timber species and treatment specification
  • Experience with your specific project type
  • Clean finish and attention to detail

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Can't provide LBP number for structural work
  • Won't specify timber treatment grade
  • No photos of finished work
  • Vague quote without specifications
