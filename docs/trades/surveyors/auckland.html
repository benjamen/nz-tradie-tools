Auckland Land Surveyors Pricing Guide

Surveyors in Auckland typically charge $1,300–$3,800 for a boundary survey, $4,500–$28,000+ for subdivision, and $900–$2,800 for a building setout. Auckland rates are the highest in NZ, reflecting the city's cost of living and high demand. Title searches and LIM reports are recommended before any boundary-related work — your surveyor can advise.

How to Choose a Land Surveyor in Auckland

  • Licensed cadastral surveyor for boundary/subdivision work
  • NZIS (NZ Institution of Surveyors) member
  • Topographic survey for resource consent
  • GPS RTK or total station equipment
  • Title plan and LINZ registration included

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Not licensed for cadastral work
  • Can't register plans with LINZ
  • Using GPS only without total station for precise work
  • No NZIS membership

Licensing Requirements

Must be licensed under the Cadastral Survey Act 2002 for cadastral work. Registration with NZIS. Verify a licence →
