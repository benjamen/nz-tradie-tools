How to Choose a Heat Pump Installer in Queenstown

  • REW registration certificate
  • HVAC-certified installer
  • Brand-authorised installer (Mitsubishi, Fujitsu etc)
  • Correct sizing calculation for your room
  • 5+ year warranty on parts and labour

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't show REW number
  • Oversizes or undersizes without explanation
  • No warranty on installation
  • Using non-brand-approved parts
