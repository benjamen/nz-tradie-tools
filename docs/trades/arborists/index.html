What to Look for When Hiring a Arborist in NZ

  • NZAA certification or ISA credentials
  • Council consent for protected or notable trees
  • Public liability insurance (minimum $1M)
  • Full clean-up and chip removal included in quote
  • Written arborist report if required

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't check council consent status
  • No insurance documentation
  • Quote includes felling but not removal
  • Not NZAA certified for complex work

Typical Costs Across NZ

Small tree removal: $500–$1,500. Large tree: $2,000–$8,000+. Stump grinding: $200–$600

Costs vary significantly by city — Auckland and Queenstown are typically the most expensive, while Invercargill and Dunedin are among the most affordable.

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing. Look for NZ Arboricultural Association (NZAA) certified arborists. Check the register →
