Whangārei Arborists & Tree Services Pricing Guide

Arborists in Whangārei typically charge $550–$1,800 for small tree removal and $2,200–$9,000+ for large trees. Stump grinding is $220–$650 per stump. Northland rates are broadly in line with the national average, with some capacity constraints for specialist trades. Ensure your arborist holds NZ Arboricultural Association accreditation and has current public liability insurance.

How to Choose a Arborist in Whangārei

  • NZAA certification or ISA credentials
  • Council consent for protected or notable trees
  • Public liability insurance (minimum $1M)
  • Full clean-up and chip removal included in quote
  • Written arborist report if required

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't check council consent status
  • No insurance documentation
  • Quote includes felling but not removal
  • Not NZAA certified for complex work
