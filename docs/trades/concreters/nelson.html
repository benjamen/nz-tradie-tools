How to Choose a Concreter in Nelson

  • NZ cement specification in quote (strength, finish)
  • Reinforcement detail (mesh, rebar)
  • Control joint pattern plan
  • Drainage design (fall direction)
  • Curing procedure included

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No control joint plan
  • Can't specify concrete strength (MPa)
  • No reinforcement mentioned
  • Won't give per m² rate

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing. LBP required for structural concrete work on buildings. Verify a licence →
