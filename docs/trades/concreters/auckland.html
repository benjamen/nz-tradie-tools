Auckland Concreters Pricing Guide

How to Choose a Concreter in Auckland

  • NZ cement specification in quote (strength, finish)
  • Reinforcement detail (mesh, rebar)
  • Control joint pattern plan
  • Drainage design (fall direction)
  • Curing procedure included

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No control joint plan
  • Can't specify concrete strength (MPa)
  • No reinforcement mentioned
  • Won't give per m² rate

Licensing Requirements

No mandatory licensing. LBP required for structural concrete work on buildings. Verify a licence →

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do concreters cost in Auckland?

Auckland concreting $120–$180/m² for a standard 100 mm residential slab, placed and finished. Always get at least 3 quotes before committing.

Do concreters need a licence in NZ?

No mandatory licensing. LBP required for structural concrete work on buildings. Check the register →

How many quotes should I get for a concreter job in Auckland?

Always get at least 3 written quotes from licensed tradespeople. Compare the scope of work, not just the price — the cheapest quote often excludes items the others include. Use our Job Cost Calculator to sense-check any quote.

What's the best way to find a reliable concreter in Auckland?

Start with Google Maps reviews (filter by highest rated, minimum 20 reviews), then check No Cowboys for verified NZ reviews. Always confirm their licence before signing anything.
