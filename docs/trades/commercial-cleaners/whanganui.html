Whanganui Commercial & Industrial Cleaners Pricing Guide

Commercial cleaners in Whanganui typically charge $32–$58/hr for office cleaning, $2–$6/m² for construction cleans, and $3–$9/m² for high-pressure washing. Whanganui rates are typically 10–15% below the national average. Confirm whether cleaning products, equipment, and waste disposal are included in the quoted rate.

How to Choose a Commercial Cleaner in Whanganui

  • Public liability insurance
  • Chemical safety data sheets for products used
  • Documented cleaning schedule
  • Police vetting for staff with facility access
  • Green/eco-certified products option

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No insurance
  • Can't provide product safety data sheets
  • No police vetting process for keyholders
  • Extremely low price (corners cut)
