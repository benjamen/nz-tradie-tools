What to Look for When Hiring a Commercial Cleaner in NZ

  • Public liability insurance
  • Chemical safety data sheets for products used
  • Documented cleaning schedule
  • Police vetting for staff with facility access
  • Green/eco-certified products option

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No insurance
  • Can't provide product safety data sheets
  • No police vetting process for keyholders
  • Extremely low price (corners cut)

Typical Costs Across NZ

Office cleaning: $30–$55/hr. Construction clean: $2–$5/m². High-pressure washing: $3–$8/m²

Costs vary significantly by city — Auckland and Queenstown are typically the most expensive, while Invercargill and Dunedin are among the most affordable.
