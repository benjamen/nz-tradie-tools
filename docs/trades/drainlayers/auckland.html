Auckland Drainlayers Pricing Guide

Auckland drainlaying $130–$180/hr; council connection fees extra; congested urban sites add cost.

Use our free Job Cost Calculator to check if a quote is reasonable for your job.

How to Choose a Drainlayer in Auckland

  • Current drainlayer licence
  • Council pre-approval for new connections
  • Drain camera inspection before any major work
  • Drainage design with fall calculations
  • Backfill and compaction specification

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Doesn't mention council approval for connections
  • No fall calculations in quote
  • Won't camera inspect existing drains
  • Can't provide licence number

Licensing Requirements

Must be registered and licensed under the Plumbers, Gasfitters and Drainlayers Act 2006. Verify a licence →
