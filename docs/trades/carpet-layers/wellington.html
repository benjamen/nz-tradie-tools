Wellington Carpet Layers & Flooring Specialists Pricing Guide

Carpet laying in Wellington typically costs $50–$120/m² supply and installed (budget to mid-range), or $15–$30/m² labour only. A 3-bedroom home (~80 m²) runs $4,000–$12,000 all-in including standard underlay. Old carpet removal adds ~$25–$65/m² extra. Most Wellington providers offer free measure and quote.

How to Choose a Carpet Layer in Wellington

  • NZ Flooring Association membership
  • Experience with your specific floor type
  • Subfloor preparation included in quote
  • Underlay specification and thickness
  • Warranty on installation workmanship

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No subfloor prep included
  • Won't specify underlay
  • Per m² quote only with no detail
  • Can't show installation photos
