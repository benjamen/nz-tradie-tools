Napier Builders & LBPs Pricing Guide

Builders in Napier typically charge $80–$100/hr for labour. New builds run $2,800–$4,200/m² depending on specifications. Heritage renovations on the city's Art Deco villas often command a premium due to specialist joinery and plasterwork. Always verify your builder's LBP licence at lbp.govt.nz before signing a contract.

How to Choose a Builder in Napier

  • Valid LBP licence (check the public register)
  • Public liability insurance (ask for a certificate)
  • References from recent similar jobs
  • Building consent experience
  • Clear written quote with inclusions and exclusions

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't provide LBP licence number
  • No written quote
  • Asks for full payment upfront
  • Can't provide references
