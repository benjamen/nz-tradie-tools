Palmerston North Builders & LBPs Pricing Guide

Builders in Palmerston North typically charge $60–$80/hr for residential labour, some of the most competitive rates in New Zealand. New builds in Manawatu average $2,200–$2,800/m². Kitchen and bathroom renovations typically run $25,000–$60,000 depending on specification. Busy demand means booking 6–12 weeks ahead is common.

How to Choose a Builder in Palmerston North

  • Valid LBP licence (check the public register)
  • Public liability insurance (ask for a certificate)
  • References from recent similar jobs
  • Building consent experience
  • Clear written quote with inclusions and exclusions

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't provide LBP licence number
  • No written quote
  • Asks for full payment upfront
  • Can't provide references
