Wellington Builders & LBPs Pricing Guide

Wellington builders charge $85–$125/hr. The hilly terrain and older housing stock (many 1900s–1960s homes) means renovations often uncover surprises. Budget a 10–15% contingency on any renovation project.

Use our free Job Cost Calculator to check if a quote is reasonable for your job.

How to Choose a Builder in Wellington

  • Valid LBP licence (check the public register)
  • Public liability insurance (ask for a certificate)
  • References from recent similar jobs
  • Building consent experience
  • Clear written quote with inclusions and exclusions

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Won't provide LBP licence number
  • No written quote
  • Asks for full payment upfront
  • Can't provide references

Licensing Requirements

LBP (Licensed Building Practitioner) licence required for restricted building work on homes and small buildings. Verify a licence →

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do builders cost in Wellington?

Wellington builders charge $85–$125/hr. The hilly terrain and older housing stock (many 1900s–1960s homes) means renovations often uncover surprises. Budget a 10–15% contingency on any renovation project. Always get at least 3 quotes before committing.

Do builders need a licence in NZ?

LBP (Licensed Building Practitioner) licence required for restricted building work on homes and small buildings. Check the register →

How many quotes should I get for a builder job in Wellington?

Always get at least 3 written quotes from licensed tradespeople. Compare the scope of work, not just the price — the cheapest quote often excludes items the others include. Use our Job Cost Calculator to sense-check any quote.

What's the best way to find a reliable builder in Wellington?

Start with Google Maps reviews (filter by highest rated, minimum 20 reviews), then check No Cowboys for verified NZ reviews. Always confirm their licence before signing anything.
