Wellington Demolition Contractors Pricing Guide

Demolition contractors in Wellington typically charge $3,500–$14,000 for an internal strip-out and $16,000–$40,000+ for a full house demolition. Asbestos removal is priced separately at $85–$220/m². Wellington rates are among the highest in NZ — budget 5–15% above the national average. Asbestos testing is mandatory before any demolition work on pre-1990 buildings.

How to Choose a Demolition Contractor in Wellington

  • Asbestos survey before any demolition on pre-2000 buildings
  • WorkSafe asbestos removal licence if required
  • Consent to demolish from council
  • Waste disposal plan with documentation
  • Neighbour and utility notification process

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No asbestos survey offered for pre-2000 buildings
  • No council consent mentioned
  • Suspiciously cheap quote (skipping asbestos process)
  • No waste manifest documentation
