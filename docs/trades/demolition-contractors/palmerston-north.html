How to Choose a Demolition Contractor in Palmerston North

  • Asbestos survey before any demolition on pre-2000 buildings
  • WorkSafe asbestos removal licence if required
  • Consent to demolish from council
  • Waste disposal plan with documentation
  • Neighbour and utility notification process

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No asbestos survey offered for pre-2000 buildings
  • No council consent mentioned
  • Suspiciously cheap quote (skipping asbestos process)
  • No waste manifest documentation
