What to Look for When Hiring a Kitchen Renovator in NZ

  • Full scope in writing (cabinetry, benchtop, appliances, plumbing, electrical)
  • 3D design plan before committing
  • Brand-specified cabinetry and benchtop products
  • Project management of all sub-trades
  • Builders guarantee / warranty

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No 3D design offered
  • Vague quote without product specifications
  • Doesn't coordinate plumbing and electrical
  • Large upfront deposit (over 30%)

Typical Costs Across NZ

Budget kitchen refresh: $15,000–$25,000. Mid-range full renovation: $30,000–$55,000. Premium: $60,000–$120,000+

Costs vary significantly by city — Auckland and Queenstown are typically the most expensive, while Invercargill and Dunedin are among the most affordable.

Licensing Requirements

LBP required for structural changes. Plumbing changes need a licensed plumber. Check the register →
