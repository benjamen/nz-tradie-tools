Hamilton Steel Fabricators & Structural Steel Pricing Guide

Steel fabricators in Hamilton typically charge $130–$260/m for structural beams installed and $400–$950/m for balustrades. Steel portal frame buildings run $38,000–$85,000+. Waikato rates are generally 10–15% below Auckland for most trades. Always verify the fabricator holds current IQP or SCNZ certification for structural work.

How to Choose a Steel Fabricator in Hamilton

  • LBP credentials for structural work
  • Steel grade specification (AS3678, Grade 300 etc)
  • Welding certification (AWS D1.1 or AS/NZS 1554)
  • Protective coating / hot-dip galvanising spec
  • Engineer-approved drawings

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • No welder certification
  • Won't specify steel grade
  • No engineer approval for structural members
  • No coating specification
