How these estimates work

Costs are based on 2026 NZ market rates drawn from our trade pricing guides, cross-referenced with industry data. All figures are GST inclusive and assume a standard residential job with reasonable site access. Prices vary based on:

  • City: Auckland and Queenstown are 10–20% above national average; smaller centres are 10–20% below.
  • Scope & complexity: Older homes, restricted access, or unusual specifications add cost.
  • Materials: Budget and premium options can differ by 2–3× for the same job.
  • Tradie availability: High-demand periods (summer, post-storm) push prices higher.

Use this calculator to sanity-check quotes and build your renovation budget. Always get 2–3 quotes before committing to a tradie.
