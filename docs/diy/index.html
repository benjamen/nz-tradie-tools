DIY Home Guides NZ

Know exactly what you can legally do yourself — and what requires a licensed tradie. Avoid fines, void insurance, and dangerous work.

Important: NZ law restricts certain electrical, plumbing, and building work to licensed tradespeople only. Doing restricted work yourself is illegal, can void your home insurance, and may create serious issues when selling. These guides clarify what's allowed — always verify with your local council or the relevant board before starting.
