Why Rates Vary by City

Auckland consistently has the highest tradie rates in New Zealand, driven by higher overheads, longer travel times, and strong demand. Wellington rates are typically 5–15% below Auckland. Christchurch and Hamilton sit in the mid-range, while smaller cities like Invercargill, Gisborne, and Whanganui tend to have the lowest rates — though smaller contractor pools can mean less competitive quotes for specialist work.

What's Included in These Rates

  • Labour only — materials, consent fees, and site costs are additional
  • Standard hours — after-hours and emergency rates are typically 50–100% higher
  • Experienced tradespeople — apprentice rates are typically 30–50% lower
  • Ex GST unless stated — add 15% for GST-registered tradies

How to Use These Rates

Use this table as a sanity check on quotes, not as a fixed price guide. A quote significantly below the typical range may indicate a less experienced operator or one cutting corners on compliance. A quote well above range warrants asking for a breakdown.
