Best Job Management Software for NZ Tradies 2026

Published 20 April 2026 · NZ Tradie Tools
softwarejob managementNZreviews

Running a tradie business without job management software in 2026 is like doing your quotes with a pencil and a stack of paper — it works, but it costs you time and money every single day.

This guide covers the best options for New Zealand tradies, with real pricing in NZD.

Why NZ Tradies Need Job Management Software

Job management software does the heavy lifting for: - Quoting and invoicing — professional quotes in minutes, not hours - Scheduling — drag-and-drop job boards, automated reminders - Time tracking — staff timesheets on mobile, no paper - Parts and materials — purchase orders, stock tracking - Customer management — job history, contact details, notes - Reporting — profitability by job, by client, by technician

The average NZ tradie business saves 8–12 hours per week by switching from paper/spreadsheets to software. At $80/hour, that's $32,000–$48,000 worth of time per year.

The Top Options for NZ Tradies

1. Fergus — Best Overall for Small to Medium Trades

Price: From $49/month + GST (1 user), scales with team size Best for: Builders, plumbers, electricians, gasfitters — any trade with 1–20 field staff

Fergus is built in New Zealand, by a New Zealander, specifically for the way Kiwi tradies work. That matters — it handles NZ-specific things like ACC, GST, PAYE, and integrates directly with Xero and MYOB.

What we like: - Purpose-built for NZ tradies (not an overseas product shoehorned in) - Excellent mobile app — works offline in rural areas - Quoting, job management, invoicing, and timesheets all in one - Customer portal so clients can see job progress - Xero and MYOB integration is seamless - Local NZ support team

Limitations: - Gets expensive with larger teams (per-user pricing) - Reporting could be deeper for enterprise users

Verdict: Best first choice for most NZ tradies.

2. Tradify — Best for Sole Traders and Small Teams

Price: From $29/month + GST (1 user) Best for: Sole traders, micro-businesses (1–5 people)

Tradify is the slickest, simplest option for smaller NZ operations. The mobile app is excellent, setup takes less than a day, and the learning curve is gentle.

What we like: - Very affordable entry price - Quick to set up and learn - Great mobile app for quoting on site - Strong Xero integration - Good for day-to-day job management

Limitations: - Less powerful than Fergus for complex projects - Purchase orders and stock management are basic

Verdict: Ideal for sole traders wanting to get organised fast.

3. Simpro — Best for Larger Operations

Price: Custom pricing (typically $200–$600+/month for 5+ users) Best for: Businesses with 10+ field staff, multiple trade types, complex projects

Simpro is enterprise-grade — powerful reporting, multi-site project management, detailed WIP tracking, and deep accounting integration. It's more complex to set up and more expensive, but the ROI is significant for larger operations.

What we like: - Extremely powerful project management - Excellent financial reporting and WIP - Handles complex multi-trade projects - Strong Australian/NZ support - Detailed job costing

Limitations: - Expensive and complex to implement - Overkill for businesses under 10 staff - Takes 2–4 weeks to get fully set up

Verdict: Best for larger, more complex tradie businesses.

4. ServiceM8 — Best for Service-Based Trades

Price: From $29/month + GST (unlimited staff, job limits per plan) Best for: Plumbers, electricians, HVAC — repeat service work

ServiceM8's model charges per job rather than per user, which suits businesses with many field staff doing high-volume service work. The scheduling board is excellent.

What we like: - Unlimited staff logins (job-based pricing) - Excellent scheduling and dispatch - Client portal and online booking - iOS-first with a great mobile experience - Good for service (break-fix) work

Limitations: - iOS only (no Android native app) - Less suited to project-based construction work

Verdict: Great for high-volume service trades, especially if your team uses iPhones.

5. Buildxact — Best for Builders and Estimators

Price: From $199/month + GST Best for: Builders, construction companies focused on accurate estimating

Buildxact is purpose-built for builders who need detailed takeoff, estimating, and project management. If you're building houses or doing large renovations, it's hard to beat.

What we like: - Digital takeoff from plans (PDF measurement tool) - Detailed estimating with supplier price books - Project management and scheduling - Client portal and variation tracking

Limitations: - More expensive than general-purpose tools - Overkill for trades that don't need detailed estimating

Verdict: The standout choice for residential builders.

  • Growing trade business (2–15 staff): Fergus
  • Large operation, complex projects: Simpro
  • Busy service trade (plumber, electrician): ServiceM8

Most offer a free trial — sign up for two or three and see which one feels right for how you work.
