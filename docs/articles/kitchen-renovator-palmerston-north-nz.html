Kitchen Renovation Palmerston North 2026 — Costs, What's Included and How to Plan

Palmerston North has a strong renovation market. The city's affordable entry-level homes often have dated kitchens — upgrading adds significant value in the Manawatu market. Here's what kitchen renovations cost in Palmerston North in 2026.

Palmerston North Kitchen Renovation Costs 2026

Kitchen type Palmerston North typical cost
Budget (flat-pack, installed) $8,000–$16,000
Mid-range (semi-custom) $16,000–$40,000
Premium (custom cabinetry) $40,000–$95,000+

All prices GST inclusive. Broadly regional.

Palmerston North Kitchen Renovation Market

Palmerston North has a strong renovation market. The city's affordable entry-level homes often have dated kitchens — upgrading adds significant value in the Manawatu market.

Tradespeople You'll Need

  • Builder / kitchen installer: Project management, demolition, cabinetry, coordination
  • Plumber: Sink, dishwasher, any plumbing relocations
  • Electrician: Oven circuit, rangehood, lighting, extra power points
  • Tiler: Splashback and floor tiles
  • Plasterer: Walls after demolition

Planning Tips

Layout changes (moving plumbing or structural walls) add cost significantly — working within the existing footprint keeps costs lower. Budget 35–45% for cabinetry, 10–20% for benchtop, 25–35% for labour.
