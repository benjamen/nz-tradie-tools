Rotorua Painting Market

Rotorua's geothermal atmosphere — hydrogen sulphide and other gases — can accelerate paint degradation, particularly on metal surfaces. Specialist paints and more frequent repainting cycles are common in geothermal-affected areas.

Exterior vs Interior Painting in Rotorua

Exterior: The preparation is everything — surface cleaning (waterblasting), filling cracks, sanding, and priming are as important as the topcoats. Skimping on prep leads to paint failure. In Rotorua, expect to repaint exteriors every 8–12 years depending on exposure.

Interior: Less weather-driven but equally dependent on preparation. Filling, sanding, and priming surfaces ensures the topcoat adheres and looks good. Quality paints (Resene, Dulux, Haymes) make a visible difference in finish and longevity.

Getting Quotes from Rotorua Painters

  • Get 2–3 written quotes for any job over $3,000
  • Confirm exactly what prep is included — filling, sanding, priming
  • Ask what paint brand and grade will be used
  • Check how many coats are included
  • Confirm scaffolding or ladder access is included for exterior work
  • Ask about the warranty on their workmanship
