How to Find a Builder in Auckland

Auckland is the busiest building market in the country, so good builders are often booked well ahead. The way to land a great one is to start early, search by area, confirm their LBP licence, and check their reviews everywhere before committing. Start in the TradieTools builder directory, filter to Auckland, and shortlist based on aggregated ratings and the type of work they specialise in.

💵 Auckland builder rates (2026): LBPs charge around $80–$110/hour, registered builders $65–$90/hour, and new home builds run $2,500–$4,500/m². Auckland sits at the top of the national range. See the full NZ builder pricing guide.

What to Look For in an Auckland Builder

Building work that needs a consent is regulated, so licensing matters. Before you hire, check:

  • LBP licence. Any restricted building work — structural, weathertightness and similar — must be done or supervised by a Licensed Building Practitioner. Verify their status on the public LBP register.
  • The right licence class. LBPs are licensed in different areas (carpentry, site, etc.), so make sure theirs covers your job.
  • Reviews across platforms. Compare NoCowboys, Google and Builderscrack together for a fuller picture than any single review gives.
  • Three written quotes. Comparing like-for-like quotes is the best defence against missing scope and padded pricing.
