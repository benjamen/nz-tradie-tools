What to Check Before You Hire

Before hiring any electrician in Hamilton:

  • EWRB (Electrical Workers Registration Board). All electrical work in NZ must be carried out by a registered electrician. Verify registration at ewrb.govt.nz.
  • Public liability insurance. At a minimum $1M cover — ask for a certificate before work starts.
  • Reviews across platforms. Check NoCowboys, Google, and Builderscrack. A consistent track record beats a single five-star review.
  • Written quote. Get the scope, rate, and any call-out fee confirmed in writing. For larger jobs, reference the Heat Pump Installation Cost NZ 2026 to benchmark the quote.
