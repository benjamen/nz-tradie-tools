Carpet Installation Cost NZ 2026 — Price Per m² & Full Room Quotes

Updated June 2026 7 min read

Carpet is still the most popular floor covering for bedrooms and living areas in NZ — warmer underfoot than hard flooring and significantly cheaper per m². In 2026, expect to pay $50–$170 per m² installed including underlay, depending on carpet grade. A full 3-bedroom home carpeting job runs $5,000–$17,000.

What's Included in a Carpet Installation Quote

  • Carpet supply — selected from sample books, ordered to your room dimensions with waste factor
  • Underlay supply — foam or felt underlay, thickness per spec
  • Gripper rods — tack strips around the room perimeter to hold carpet in place
  • Installation labour — cutting, stretching, joining if needed, door bar fitting
  • Old carpet removal — often quoted separately at $3–$6 per m² (or $80–$150 per room)
  • Waste and disposal — tip fees for old carpet removal

FAQ — Carpet Installation Costs NZ

How much does carpet installation cost in NZ?

Labour only (if you supply the carpet): $15–$25 per m². Full supply and install with standard underlay: $50–$185 per m² depending on carpet grade. Old carpet removal adds $3–$6/m² or $80–$150 per room.

What is the cheapest carpet option in NZ?

Budget polyester carpet from major retailers (Carpet Court, The Flooring Room, Elwood Carpets) starts at around $25–$35/m² supply-only, or $50–$70/m² installed with basic underlay. It's suitable for rentals and low-traffic rooms but won't last as long as nylon or wool.

How long does carpet last in NZ?

Budget polyester: 8–12 years with moderate traffic. Mid-range nylon: 12–18 years. Quality NZ wool: 20–25 years. Proper underlay, professional installation, and regular vacuuming significantly extend carpet life.

Do I need to move furniture for carpet installation?

Yes — all furniture must be moved before the installer arrives. Most carpet companies will move light furniture (beds, sofas, chairs) as part of the installation but won't move heavy appliances, pianos, or bookshelves full of books. Confirm what's included when booking.
