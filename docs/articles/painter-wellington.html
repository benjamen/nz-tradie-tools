How to Find a Painter in Wellington

The fastest way to find a reliable painter in Wellington is to search by suburb, confirm their credentials, and compare ratings across platforms before you call. Browse the Wellington painter directory on TradieTools, shortlist two or three based on their reviews, and get written quotes before committing.

💵 Wellington painter rates (2026): Expect $45–$70/hr (or $25–$45/m² for walls). Wellington rates are the second-highest in NZ after Auckland. Budget for the top end of the national range. See the full NZ Painter Cost Guide 2026 for a complete breakdown by job type.

What to Check Before You Hire

Before hiring any painter in Wellington:

  • NZ Master Painters (industry body — not a legal requirement but a quality signal). Painting is not regulated in NZ but membership of NZ Master Painters indicates professional standards and insurance.
  • Public liability insurance. At a minimum $1M cover — ask for a certificate before work starts.
  • Reviews across platforms. Check NoCowboys, Google, and Builderscrack. A consistent track record beats a single five-star review.
  • Written quote. Get the scope, rate, and any call-out fee confirmed in writing. For larger jobs, reference the Home Renovation Cost Guide NZ 2026 to benchmark the quote.
