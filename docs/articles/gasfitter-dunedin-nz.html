Gasfitters Dunedin 2026 — Gas Fitting Rates, Costs and What Requires a Licensed Gasfitter

Dunedin uses a mix of natural gas and LPG. The city's cold winters drive strong demand for gas heating — gas fires and log burners with gas backup are popular in Dunedin's older homes. Here's what gasfitters charge in Dunedin in 2026.

Dunedin Gas Supply

Natural gas is available in parts of Dunedin via First Gas. LPG is used where mains gas isn't connected.

What Requires a Licensed Gasfitter in NZ

All gasfitting must be done by someone registered with the PGDB (Plumbers, Gasfitters and Drainlayers Board):

  • Connecting any gas appliance (hob, oven, heater, hot water, BBQ point)
  • Installing or modifying gas pipework
  • LPG to natural gas conversions
  • Testing gas systems after any work

Verify at: pgdb.co.nz — never allow unlicensed gasfitting.
