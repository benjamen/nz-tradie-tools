## Palmerston North Painting Market

Palmerston North's windy climate accelerates exterior paint weathering. The 'Windy City' reputation means quality preparation and paint selection are especially important for lasting results.

## Exterior vs Interior Painting in Palmerston North

**Exterior:** The preparation is everything — surface cleaning (waterblasting), filling cracks, sanding, and priming are as important as the topcoats. Skimping on prep leads to paint failure. In Palmerston North, expect to repaint exteriors every 8–12 years depending on exposure.

**Interior:** Less weather-driven but equally dependent on preparation. Filling, sanding, and priming surfaces ensures the topcoat adheres and looks good. Quality paints (Resene, Dulux, Haymes) make a visible difference in finish and longevity.

## Getting Quotes from Palmerston North Painters

- Get 2–3 written quotes for any job over $3,000
- Confirm exactly what prep is included — filling, sanding, priming
- Ask what paint brand and grade will be used
- Check how many coats are included
- Confirm scaffolding or ladder access is included for exterior work
- Ask about the warranty on their workmanship
