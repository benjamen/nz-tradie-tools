## Rotorua Plumbing Market

Rotorua's geothermal activity can affect plumbing — some areas have acidic groundwater or geothermal gases that can corrode standard pipe materials. Local plumbers with geothermal experience are preferred.

Geothermal activity in Rotorua has unique implications for building — some areas have geothermal ground conditions that affect foundations, drainage, and pipe materials. Always check geothermal hazard zoning before buying or building.

## What Requires a Licensed Plumber in NZ

Sanitary plumbing and drainlaying are restricted trades — all work must be done by someone registered with the PGDB:

- Water supply, drainage, and sanitary systems
- Hot water cylinder installation
- Bathroom and kitchen plumbing
- Connections to the public sewer
