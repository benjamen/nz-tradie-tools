Growing strongly as Aucklanders relocate for lifestyle and affordability. Construction and renovation demand is high.

## Getting the Best Paint Job in Whangarei

Quality preparation is 30–40% of a good paint job. A painter who washes, sands, fills, and primes properly will produce a job that lasts 10+ years. Always ask what preparation is included in your quote — shortcuts here mean the job fails in 3–5 years.

For exteriors in Whangarei's subtropical, warm and humid with high rainfall climate, use quality exterior paint — Resene X-200 or Dulux Weathershield are proven NZ products.
