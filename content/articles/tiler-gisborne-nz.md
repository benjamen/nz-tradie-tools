## Waterproofing in Wet Areas

All shower and bathroom tiling must be done over a waterproofed substrate — tiles alone don't waterproof. NZ Building Code requires a compliant waterproof membrane in wet areas to minimum 1,800mm height. A good Gisborne tiler will either waterproof themselves or coordinate with a builder to ensure compliance before tiling begins.

## Tile Selection Tips

- **Shower floor:** Choose R10+ slip-rated matt or textured tiles
- **Large format (600mm+):** Stunning but needs a very flat substrate and adds labour cost
- **Rectified tiles:** Machine-cut edges allow 1–2mm grout lines for a seamless finish
