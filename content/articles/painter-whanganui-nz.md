Heritage home renovation is big in Whanganui — the city has beautiful Victorian and Edwardian stock. New builds are increasing on the city's fringes.

## Getting the Best Paint Job in Whanganui

Quality preparation is 30–40% of a good paint job. A painter who washes, sands, fills, and primes properly will produce a job that lasts 10+ years. Always ask what preparation is included in your quote — shortcuts here mean the job fails in 3–5 years.

For exteriors in Whanganui's mild, moderate rainfall, warm summers climate, use quality exterior paint — Resene X-200 or Dulux Weathershield are proven NZ products.
