## Invercargill Painting Market

Steady residential market with strong demand for insulation, heating, and weathertightness work. Affordable property prices drive renovation activity.

## Getting the Best Paint Job in Invercargill

Quality preparation is 30–40% of a good paint job. A painter who washes, sands, fills, and primes properly will produce a job that lasts 10+ years. Always ask what preparation is included in your quote — shortcuts here mean the job fails in 3–5 years.

For exteriors in Invercargill's cold, wet, and windy — one of NZ's coldest cities climate, use quality exterior paint — Resene X-200 or Dulux Weathershield are proven NZ products.
