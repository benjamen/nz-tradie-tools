## Palmerston North Building Market

Palmerston North's affordable land prices attract first-home buyers and investors, supporting new builds and renovations. Massey University's ongoing campus development also sustains commercial building activity.

Palmerston North has a mix of older post-war homes and newer subdivisions on the city's edges. The relatively affordable housing market has driven renovation activity as buyers improve older stock.

## LBP Licensing

All restricted building work requires a Licensed Building Practitioner (LBP). Verify at [lbp.govt.nz](https://www.lbp.govt.nz). Your builder must provide a Record of Work on completion.

**How much does a builder cost in Palmerston North?**
