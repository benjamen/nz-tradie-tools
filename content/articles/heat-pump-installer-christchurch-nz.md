## Christchurch Heat Pump Market

Christchurch's cold winters — including regular frosts — make heat pumps essential. Cold-climate models rated to -15°C are strongly recommended. The city's flat terrain and single-storey housing makes installation straightforward.

## Choosing a Heat Pump for Christchurch

**Capacity:** Size matters — too small won't heat the room; too large short-cycles and wastes energy. A qualified installer will calculate the right kW for your room size, insulation, and ceiling height.

**Cold-climate rating:** Check the COP (Coefficient of Performance) at low temperatures. For Christchurch, look for units rated to at least -10°C (ideally -15°C) for reliable winter performance.

**Brands in NZ:** Mitsubishi Electric, Daikin, Fujitsu, Panasonic, and LG are the main brands with full NZ dealer and service networks. All offer comparable performance — installer quality and warranty support matter as much as brand.

**Installation:** All heat pump electrical work must be done by an EWRB-registered electrician. The refrigerant work must be done by an MTA-certified refrigeration technician. Most heat pump companies provide both in one team.

## Heat Pump Servicing in Christchurch

Heat pumps should be serviced every 1–2 years:
- Filter clean (you can do this yourself every 3 months)
- Coil clean (requires professional equipment)
- Refrigerant check
- Drain check

Annual service cost in Christchurch: $800–$1,800. Regular servicing maintains efficiency and extends unit life.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does heat pump installation cost in Christchurch?**
Single split system (2.5–3.5kW): $2,500–$4,500 supply and installed. Multi-split (2 rooms): $4,500–$8,000.

**What size heat pump do I need for Christchurch?**
A rough guide: 1kW per 10m² of well-insulated floor space. A 25m² lounge needs roughly 2.5kW. Your installer will calculate based on your specific home — get a site assessment before buying.

**How often should I service my heat pump in Christchurch?**
Filter: clean every 3 months yourself. Full professional service: every 1–2 years. Cost: $800–$1,800.
