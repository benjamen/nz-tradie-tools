## LBP Licensing

All restricted building work requires a Licensed Building Practitioner (LBP). Verify at [lbp.govt.nz](https://www.lbp.govt.nz). Your builder must provide a Record of Work on completion.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does a builder cost in Nelson?**
Hourly: $85–$155/hr. New build: $2,600–$4,600/m² turnkey. Extensions typically cost $2,800–$5,000/m².

**Do I need building consent in Nelson?**
Most structural work and new builds require consent from the Nelson-Tasman Council. Your builder should advise.
