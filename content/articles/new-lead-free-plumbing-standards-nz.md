# New Lead-Free Plumbing Standards: What NZ Plumbers Need to Know

A significant update to the New Zealand Building Code took effect on May 2, 2026. If you are a plumber, drainlayer, or contractor involved in residential builds or renovations that require building consent, these changes affect the materials you specify and install.

## What’s Changing?

All plumbing products used for drinking water—such as pipes, valves, and fittings—must now meet strict new criteria:
1. **Lead-Free:** Materials must be lead-free, significantly reducing the potential for lead contamination in water supplies.
2. **Dezincification-Resistant:** Products must be resistant to dezincification, ensuring the longevity and integrity of plumbing systems over time.

## Why This Matters

These updates are a crucial step towards enhancing public health and water quality across New Zealand. For the plumbing trade, this represents a move towards higher quality, more durable infrastructure.

## How to Stay Compliant

Compliance starts with product selection. 
- **Verify before you buy:** Before ordering components for a new consent, check the manufacturer’s documentation to confirm the products meet these updated Building Code requirements.
- **Update your suppliers:** Ensure your local plumbing merchant understands your requirements. Ask for documentation of compliance for all plumbing products you purchase for drinking water systems.
- **Maintain records:** As you manage these new requirements, keep detailed records of the products you install in each project. Using digital tools like [Fastcrew](https://fastcrew.nz) is an efficient way to store product specifications, warranties, and compliance certificates against specific jobs. Having this information organized not only makes you look professional but also protects your business in the event of any future audits or compliance questions.

- **Communicate with clients:** Explain why you’ve selected specific materials. Educating clients on *why* these materials are safer and more durable adds value to your service.
