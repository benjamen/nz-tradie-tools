## Do I Need a Consent to Remove a Tree in Whangarei?

Tree removal rules vary by Northland Council zone and whether trees are listed or in a notable tree schedule. **Always check with Northland Council before removing trees** — fines for removing protected trees can be substantial. A qualified arborist will know local rules and can advise.

**Certified arborists:** Look for NZ Arboriculture Association (NZAA) members or ISA (International Society of Arboriculture) certified arborists for complex or high-risk work.
