## The Problem with Chasing Work

Most tradies in New Zealand get their first few jobs through word of mouth. That works well when you're starting out and your network is fresh. But after a few years, referrals slow down, and the gap gets filled by whatever time-consuming alternative you can find: Facebook, local noticeboards, random directories that haven't been updated since 2018.

The issue with reactive platforms — Facebook groups, community pages — is that the timing is backwards. You're responding to posts, competing with whoever else sees the same request, and the homeowner is usually choosing on price alone because they don't know how to evaluate quality.

Aaron wanted to flip that: be visible to homeowners before they post, not after.
