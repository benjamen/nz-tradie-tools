## Consent Requirements in Whangarei

Boundary fences up to 2.0m generally don't require consent in most residential zones. Pool fences must comply with NZ Building Code (Clause F9) regardless of height. Front boundary fences in some zones have height restrictions. Always check with Northland Council for your specific zone.
